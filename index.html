Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding our products, installation, and services.

What sizes do your HDPE Pipes come in?

Our pipes range from 20mm to 1000mm in OD. Custom sizes available upon request.

Do you offer custom lengths and colors?

Yes, we offer coils up to 1000 meters for smaller diameters and standard lengths for larger ones.

What does PE100 mean?

PE100 is a high-density polyethylene grade that provides excellent hydrostatic strength.

Are these pipes UV resistant?

Yes, our black HDPE pipes are completely UV resistant.

How are the pipes joined?

Joined using butt fusion, electrofusion, or mechanical compression fittings.
